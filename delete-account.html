Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This page explains how you can request deletion of your MOC Books account and the data associated with it. MOC Books is operated by MOC Technologies.

1. HOW TO REQUEST DELETION

To request that your MOC Books account and its associated data be deleted, send an email from the email address linked to your account to support@mocbooks.com.ng with the subject line Account Deletion Request.

In your email, please include:

  • The name on your MOC Books account
  • The phone number or email address used to log in

We use these details only to confirm your identity and locate the correct account. We process verified requests within 30 days and confirm by email once your account has been deleted.

2. WHAT DATA IS DELETED

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the following from our systems:

  • Your account profile (name, phone number, email)
  • Your business records stored in the app, including inventory items, sales records, payment records, and customer records you created
  • Any staff accounts linked to your account
  • App settings and preferences associated with your account

3. WHAT DATA MAY BE KEPT, AND FOR HOW LONG

After an account is deleted, we may retain a limited amount of data only where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Records we are legally required to keep, retained only for the period required by applicable law
  • Routine backup copies, which are overwritten on a rolling basis and fully cleared within 90 days

Retained data is kept secure and is not used for any other purpose. Once the applicable retention period ends, this data is permanently deleted.

4. DELETING INDIVIDUAL RECORDS WITHOUT CLOSING YOUR ACCOUNT

You do not have to delete your whole account to remove data. Within the app, an owner can delete individual customers, sales, and inventory items at any time. Deleting your account removes everything described above.
